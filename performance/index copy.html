Accessibility:

is the degree to which a product, device, service, or environment is available to as many people as possible.

Accessibility can be viewed as the "ability to access" and benefit from some system or entity.

The concept often focuses on people with disabilities or special needs (such as the Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities) and their right of access, enabling the use of assistive technology.
