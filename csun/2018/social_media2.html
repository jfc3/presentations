Creating an Accessibility Community and Keeping it Going

Social Media to Help Build Community Cont’d

  • Danger is most people using social media are advanced users/geeks
  • Don’t forget about other communications, such as:
    • E-mail discussion groups
    • Online forums
    • Face-to-face meetings, especially to reach people with disabilities
  • As digital accessibility professionals we need to “go to” the mainstream high-tech community
  • Communicate with professionalism and never assume someone knows the digital accessibility basics
