Creating an Accessibility Community and Keeping it Going

Are You an Independent Accessibility Person or Want to Volunteer?

  • Get help with events as soon as you can
  • Team is important
    • Need to know others can help when necessary
    • Sometimes you’re the one who’s helping
  • Time management is HUGE
    • You have to be able to fit everything in
    • Don’t forget things like family, laundry, “me” time
