Creating an Accessibility Community and Keeping it Going

What an accessibility community can be

  • Two or more people talking accessibility at the any of the following places:
    • Coffee shop
    • At lunch or dinner or drinks
    • Public libraries
    • Colleges
    • Company or government offices
    • Over social media like Twitter, Facebook, or whatever the cool kids are using
  • Monthly meetings for drinks or talks
  • Annual events like Accessibility Camp's
