Creating an Accessibility Community and Keeping it Going

What happens when you live out in the middle of nowhere

  • Use the internet and leveraged your existing network
    • Meetup.com
    • Eventbrite.com
    • Twitter
    • Facebook
  • Provide ample notice
  • Contacted local NFB (National Federation of the Blind) and ACB (American Council of the Blind) chapters, public libraries, etc.
  • Promise free refreshments if possible but don't worry if you can't
  • Encourage those who attend to bring content/unique experience to the table
